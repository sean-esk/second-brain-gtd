# START HERE - Your First Week Guide

Welcome to your Second Brain! This guide will walk you through your first week to help you build the habit and understand the workflow.

## üìÖ Day-by-Day First Week

### Day 1: Setup & First Captures

- [ ] **Completed `/setup`** (if you haven't already)
- [ ] **First captures:** Use `/capture` 3-5 times today
  - Capture any current tasks on your mind
  - Capture any ideas or thoughts
  - Capture things you need to do this week
  - **Don't organize - just capture!**

**What you should have by end of Day 1:**
- Personal context file created
- 1-2 projects created during setup
- 3-5 captures in your inbox (`00-Inbox/Daily/`)

---

### Day 2: First Processing

- [ ] **Run `/process-inbox`** for the first time
  - Answer any clarifying questions about vague captures
  - Watch as items get organized into projects/tasks
  - See how GTD clarifying questions work
  - Your inbox should hit zero!

- [ ] **More captures:** Use `/capture` 2-3 times today

**What you should have by end of Day 2:**
- Inbox processed to zero
- Captures organized into projects/tasks
- Understanding of how processing works

---

### Day 3-4: Daily Planning Rhythm

- [ ] **Morning: Run `/daily-plan`**
  - Answer questions about energy, context, time
  - Get your prioritized task list
  - Work from the plan!

- [ ] **Throughout day: `/capture`** as thoughts come up

- [ ] **Evening: Run `/daily-closeout`**
  - Review what you accomplished
  - Tomorrow's draft plan gets created
  - Feel good about your progress!

**What you're building:**
- Morning planning habit
- Capture throughout day
- Evening reflection

---

### Day 5-7: Complete the Loop

- [ ] **Continue daily rhythm:**
  - Morning: `/daily-plan`
  - Throughout: `/capture`
  - Evening: `/daily-closeout`

- [ ] **Second `/process-inbox`** (mid-week)
  - Clear your captures
  - Review your projects
  - Keep inbox at zero

- [ ] **Third `/process-inbox`** (end of week)
  - Weekly processing
  - Project review
  - Archive any completed projects

**What you should have by end of Week 1:**
- Established daily rhythm
- Inbox processed 2-3 times
- Completed some tasks
- Feeling more organized!

## üö® Common First Week Mistakes

### Mistake: Not Capturing Enough
**Problem:** Trying to organize in your head instead of using the system
**Solution:** Capture EVERYTHING, even tiny thoughts. Trust the system.

### Mistake: Trying to Organize While Capturing
**Problem:** Spending time categorizing during `/capture`
**Solution:** Just get it down! `/process-inbox` will organize it later.

### Mistake: Skipping Processing
**Problem:** Inbox fills up, you lose trust in the system
**Solution:** Process 3x this week (even if inbox is small)

### Mistake: Perfectionism
**Problem:** Trying to make perfect notes or perfect projects
**Solution:** Done is better than perfect. Refine over time.

### Mistake: Not Running Daily Closeout
**Problem:** Miss reflection and tomorrow prep
**Solution:** Make it a 5-minute ritual. Non-negotiable.
